ABOUT

Hey there! I’m Meaghan Socaciu, an Arizona born and raised graphic designer and illustrator.

I have experience designing and coding for web, video/animation, and print. Projects where technology and design intersect interest me most. I believe it’s important to stay curious, keep learning, and exploring what’s new. That is what makes me excited about design.

When I’m not eating, breathing or sleeping design, you can find me konmari-ing my wardrobe, baking sweets, or playing video games. When I can, I love to travel and explore the vast world around me. I am always developing my design skills and love to experiment in all aspects of visual communication.
