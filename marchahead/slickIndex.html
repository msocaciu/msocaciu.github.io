MARCH
AHEAD

March Ahead is a MLK celebration campaign developed by design team Konstrukt for Arizona State University (ASU) to promote student leadership and volunteerism at ASU.

This campaign was inspired by the pickets signs of the civil rights movement.

From our concept, we developed a series of posters, an evite, and a mobile responsive website.
